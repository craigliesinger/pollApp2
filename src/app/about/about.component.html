Vybe Survey - BETA

Have you ever given a speech or presentation to a group of people and posed a question to the group at large only to get silence and blank faces in return?

Use Vybe Survey to eliminate these moments.

Vybe Survey is an audience engagement tool designed to benefit both presenter and attendee.


ENGAGE

The host can pose open ended and multiple choice questions in real time, and the entire crowd can see and explore the collective answers.





READ THE ROOM

Attendees have the ability to subtly and anonymously give general feedback to the presenter with the Vybe Meter.

The host sees the collective average, thus letting him or her know instantly if they are losing their crowd or if they are preaching to the choir.





ANALYZE

Sentiment analysis on open text questions provides meaningful analysis on qualitative data.
